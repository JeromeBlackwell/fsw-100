Directions

Wharf Marina in Fells Point 1234 Alicanna Street Baltimore, Maryland 54321

It is best to get to the Marina at least 15 min before departure time. There is free parking lot inside the Marina. When you get to end of Alicianna St, you will be at Henderson Wharf Hotel and Marina. There is a gate to get into the Marina. DO NOT BLOCK THE GATE. If we are not at the gate to let you in, Please wait in the circle by the hotel and call Capt Don on the phone at (410)123-4567.

I-95 South (Washington DC)

Follow 95 North to Baltimore. Take exit 53 (395 North). Approximately 1/4 mile, turn onto Pratt St (This is a one way street). Follow Pratt past the Inner Harbor Attractions. At President Street, turn Right. Stay on President Street. Follow President a few blocks and turn left on Eastern Ave. Follow Eastern you will pass Broadway go a few more streets and turn right on Wolfe St. Follow Wolfe (one way) till it ends, it will turn into Alicianna Street for the last block. You will see Henderson Warf Hotel. Wait at the circle in front of the hotel and we will come up and let you into the Marina.
