About US

Platnuim Fishing Charters is family-owned and operated. All of our captains are United States Coast Guard licensed and certified operators. Our three generations of fishing guides have been fishing and cruising the Chesapeake Bay for numerous years to provide you with the experience it takes to make your outing safe, memorable and most of all PRODUCTIVE. Let us be at your service!

We take pride in being very knowledgeable in all phases and styles of fishing in the Chesapeake Bay and its tributaries, ensuring our customers have the greatest chance and advantage possible to catch those fish. We enjoy teaching first-time fishermen just as much as having those more experienced anglers and fulfilling all of the expectations you have of your charter excursion. We want to catch those fish probably more than you do!

Platnuim Fishing Charters is the, "Only Full Time Fishing Charter" out of Baltimore's Inner Harbor in Fells Point Maryland.
